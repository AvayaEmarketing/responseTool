PBX
1) Do you currently have a PBX in your company? Is it owned or rented?

PBX Age
2) How old is the PBX?

PBX Brand and PBX version
3) Which brand is the PBX? Do you know the model? Is it TDM or IP?

Extensions
"4) How many extensions do you have in your phone system? How many trunks do you have? Do you have analog phones, digital phones or IP phones?"

Locations / Branches
5) Do you have other locations? Are they connected? Are they connected only through Data or also through voice?
Satisfaction
"6) Does the PBX satisfy your current needs? (They may have changed the way they work or expanded in number of extensions and the PBX is not capable to go with the change) "

Employees
"7) How many employees do you have in this location? How many employees do you have in the whole company? Do you have mobile employees? How many? "

Carrier
8) Which is your carrier?

Industry
9) Which is the main activity in the company?



10) Do you have a Contact Center in the company?

-YES:
A - How many agents do you have?

B - What kind of service do you have in the Contact Center? (Outbound calls, inbound calls, Customer care, sales, etc.)

C - What kind of report management software do you have? How do you measure your customer satisfaction and productivity?

Do you have an IVR?

D – Are you interested in adding other ways of contact: email, SMS, etc.?

- NO:
Verify if they outsource the Call center and if they are interested in stopping the outsource to integrate the CC to their phone system.



11) Do you currently have Unified Communications?
NO: Are you interested in the possibility of integrating your PBX:
a- with your mobile phone? With your voicemail?
b- With your mail system?
c- With the remote users or mobile users to increase the productivity and cut costs?


Maintenance Service (Only Clients)
12) It has a maintenance contract? Is it directly with Avaya or through one of our business partner? Which partner would that be?
Do you know what this contract covers?
Are the response times the expected? What about the quality of care and service?


13) Which brand are your switches and/or routers?
-Do you have an standard in the brand of this equipments?
-How many switches / routers you have and with what capability (Core Switches; Edge Switches; how many ports are there?)
-How old is the networking equipment?
-What is the speed of your equipments? (10 megabits; 100 megabits; 1000 Megabits/1 GB)
-Do your equipments have QoS (Quality of service)? [QoS is a feature of routers and Switches wich prioritizes traffic so that more important traffic can pass first]
-How many equipments you have to connect to the net (PC's, IP Phones, security camaras) ?
-Do you have a need for acquisition or upgrading of these equipments?
-In case of acquisition, do you require instalation/configuration service ofr the new equipments?
-In case of acquisition, do you feel interested in any of these services?:
a- Parts replacement service: (inmediate parts replacement, in a few hours); or you can get:
b- Warranty service ( replacement within 45 to 60 days)

please provide an estimated range of assigned budget that you may have/ may get:"

From U$S 5.000 to 10.000 (five thousands to ten thousands dollars)
From U$S 10.000 to 30.000 (ten thousands to thirty thousands dollars)
More than U$S 50.000 (more than fifty thousands dollars)


2- AUTHORITY:
"Is the contact the primary decision maker for IT purchases? Who would be the final decision maker?
(include every decision maker's name as provided)"
If not or not the aconomic desition maker or in case that there are othe desition makers involved, inform the name of this person and title (buyer, finances, etc.)

Have you defined any specific requirements as well?
Do you currently have any global or local telecommunications Project? Like:
PBX Upgrade.
IP Telephony implementation.
Deployment or expansion in your Call Center.
Mobility Solutions and Unified Messaging.
Data
Maintenance service


Detail and explain the project, what’s the main idea and reason
(I.E.; IP telephony to reduce costs in calls, etc)

Do you think you can start the Project implementation/deployment in…?
0 to 30 days
1 to 3 months
3 to 6 months
7 to 12 months (not eligible)
1 year (not eligible)
